Hi! I am a fourth year PhD Candidate at the ACME Lab ( formerly a member of the THING Lab ) at the ATLAS Institute at the University of Colorado Boulder. I research methods and tools to facilitate participatory design of Human Robot Interactions (HRI). While exploring these tools/techniques I aim to enrich design partners' socio-emotional well-being. For instance, through: telepresent play, stress micro-interventions, personal expression, and informal learning.
